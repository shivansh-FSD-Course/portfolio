Introduction

About

I am a Master's student in Software Engineering at LUT University, Finland, pursuing research at the intersection of human behavior and artificial intelligence. My work explores how trust forms, evolves, and sometimes breaks in our interactions with intelligent systems.

As Head Researcher on the MAISA project—a €500,000 Business Finland initiative—I lead investigations into human-AI collaboration within software engineering contexts. This work combines rigorous experimental methodology with physiological computing techniques to understand the unconscious dimensions of human-machine interaction.

I believe that the future of AI lies not in replacing human judgment, but in augmenting it thoughtfully, ethically, and with deep respect for human autonomy.
